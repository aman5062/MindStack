Privacy Policy — Mindstack

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Mindstack ("the app") is a 100% offline, local-only personal notes / "second brain" application. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Summary

Mindstack does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device. The released app has no internet permission and makes no network connections.

Data We Store (On Your Device Only)

All content you create — notes, ideas, reminders, voice recordings, and any backups you export — is stored locally on your device in a private SQLite database at com.brainvault.offline.

This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not sent to us or to any third party.
  • Is not accessible to other apps.
  • Is not backed up to the cloud by the app (Android auto-backup is disabled).

Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions, each used only for its stated on-device feature:

  • Notifications — to show your local reminders.
  • Microphone (Record Audio) — only when you record a voice note. Recordings are saved locally and never uploaded.
  • Biometrics (Fingerprint/Face) — optional, to unlock the app. Biometric data is handled entirely by the Android system; the app never sees or stores it.
  • Vibrate / Wake Lock / Receive Boot Completed — to deliver reminders reliably, including re-arming them after a device restart.

The released app does not request the Internet permission.

Security

  • App-lock PINs are stored only as a SHA-256 hash, never in plain text.
  • Optional encrypted backups are protected using your PIN.
  • Biometric unlock relies on your device's secure biometric APIs.

Because all data is stored locally, protecting your device (screen lock, encryption, physical security) is the primary safeguard for your data. If you uninstall the app or wipe its data, your content is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last Updated" date.
